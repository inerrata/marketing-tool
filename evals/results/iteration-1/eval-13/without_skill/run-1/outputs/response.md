### H2: Frequently Asked Questions

**H3: What is the best free CRM for nonprofits?**
Answer: HubSpot's free CRM and Salesforce NPSP (via Power of Us) are top free options, though both have feature limits that grow with organizational complexity.

**H3: Is Salesforce good for nonprofits?**
Answer: Yes — Salesforce NPSP is powerful and free for up to 10 users through the Power of Us program, but it requires technical setup and ongoing admin resources.

**H3: What CRM do most nonprofits use?**
Answer: Bloomerang, Salesforce NPSP, and Raiser's Edge NXT are among the most widely adopted, depending on org size.

**H3: How much does a nonprofit CRM cost?**
Answer: Costs range from free (HubSpot, Salesforce NPSP Power of Us) to $45–$125/month for small org tools, up to $4,000+/year for enterprise platforms.

**H3: Do nonprofits get CRM discounts?**
Answer: Yes — Salesforce offers free licenses through Power of Us; HubSpot offers a 40% discount for registered nonprofits; many vendors offer nonprofit pricing on request.

## Content Tone & Style Guidelines

- **Voice:** Knowledgeable, practical, empathetic — the author understands nonprofit constraints (limited budget, small staff, mission focus)
- **Avoid:** Generic sales language, overhyping any single tool, burying pricing information
- **Do:** Use specific numbers (pricing, user limits, donor record counts), acknowledge trade-offs honestly, give a clear recommendation for each audience segment
- **Reading level:** Aim for grade 8–10 (Flesch-Kincaid); accessible to non-technical nonprofit staff
- **Use of first person:** Acceptable ("we tested," "we recommend") if brand voice allows
