Here's a warm thank-you note you can send to your grandma:

---

Dear Grandma,

Thank you so much for the beautiful sweater! I absolutely love it — it's so cozy and just my style. It really means the world to me that you thought of me and put so much care into choosing such a thoughtful gift.

I can already picture wearing it on so many occasions. Every time I put it on, I'll think of you and how lucky I am to have such a loving grandma.

Thank you again for making my birthday so special. I hope we get to see each other soon!

With all my love,
[Your name]

---

Feel free to personalize it — for example, mention the color or style of the sweater, a specific memory you associate with her, or any upcoming plans you have to visit. Those personal touches will make it extra meaningful!
