Here are 3 subject line options for a Black Friday email from a coffee subscription brand:

1. **"Black Friday Deal: 30% Off Your Coffee Subscription — Today Only"**
   - Clear, direct, and urgency-driven. Works well for a broad audience that responds to discounts and deadlines.

2. **"Your best mornings are about to get cheaper ☕"**
   - Softer sell with a lifestyle angle. Appeals to loyal subscribers who connect emotionally with their daily coffee ritual.

3. **"We only do this once a year. Black Friday is here."**
   - Builds exclusivity and anticipation. Great for brands with a premium or indie identity that doesn't discount often.
