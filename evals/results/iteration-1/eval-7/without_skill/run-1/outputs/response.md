# B2B Fintech Startup Content Calendar
## 12-Week Rolling Content Plan

---

### Assumptions Made
- **Company stage:** Early-to-growth stage B2B fintech startup
- **Product:** A financial operations or payments platform for SMBs or mid-market companies
- **Goals:** Build brand awareness, generate qualified leads, establish thought leadership
- **Channels:** LinkedIn (primary), Email newsletter, Company blog/website, Twitter/X
- **Team capacity:** 1–2 content contributors, publishing 3–4 pieces per week across channels
- **Audience:** CFOs, finance managers, operations leads, founders at SMBs and mid-market firms

## Distribution & Amplification Strategy

**LinkedIn:**
- Post from both company page and founder/team personal pages
- Engage with every comment within the first hour of posting
- Cross-tag customers, partners, and investors where relevant
- Aim for 3–5 posts/week from company page; 2–3 from founder

**Email:**
- Segment list: prospects, customers, investors/partners
- Send frequency: 1x per week (Wednesday or Thursday for best open rates)
- A/B test subject lines on any list over 500 subscribers
- Track: open rate (target >35%), CTR (target >4%)

**Blog/SEO:**
- Focus on bottom-of-funnel and mid-funnel keywords
- Suggested clusters: "AP automation for startups," "finance stack for SMBs," "month-end close automation"
- Internally link all posts to product demo pages
- Repurpose every blog post into 2–3 LinkedIn posts

**Twitter/X:**
- Post threads for maximum reach; hook in first line is critical
- Engage with fintech community and relevant hashtags: #fintech #CFO #startupfinance #B2Bfinance

## Quick-Start Recommendations

1. **Start with the founder's story** — personal posts from founders consistently outperform brand posts by 5–10x on LinkedIn.
2. **Launch an email list on Day 1** — even if small, a direct-owned channel compounds over time.
3. **Document one real customer win early** — a concrete case study will be your highest-converting asset.
4. **Batch content production weekly** — create all posts for the week in one sitting to maintain consistency.
5. **Repurpose aggressively** — each long-form blog post should generate at least 3 LinkedIn posts, 1 email, and 2–3 tweets.
