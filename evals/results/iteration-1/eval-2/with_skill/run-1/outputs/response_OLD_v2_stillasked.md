Before I write your homepage hero, three quick questions so the copy actually speaks to your users rather than sounding like every other scheduling tool:

1. **Who is this for, specifically?** For example: solo content creators, social media managers at agencies, small business owners posting for themselves? The narrower the picture, the sharper the copy.

2. **What's the one thing your tool does better than the alternatives?** (e.g. fastest to set up, supports the most networks, AI-assisted captions, best for a specific platform like LinkedIn or TikTok — whatever makes someone choose you over Buffer, Hootsuite, or just posting manually.)

3. **What do you want visitors to do on the homepage?** Start a free trial, sign up for a waitlist, book a demo?

Once I have those, I'll write you a headline, subheadline, and CTA — ready to drop in.
