Assuming your audience is small-to-mid-size businesses or solo creators who manage their own social presence; adjust if you're targeting agencies or enterprise teams.

Here are three options for your homepage hero section, each taking a different angle. Every option includes a headline, subheadline, and primary CTA.

---

**Option 1 — Direct benefit (clearest, highest-converting for cold traffic)**

**Headline:** Schedule a week of social posts in 20 minutes.

**Subheadline:** [Tool name] plans, schedules, and publishes your content across every channel — so you show up consistently without living on your phone.

**CTA:** Start scheduling free

---

**Option 2 — Problem-led (works when the pain is well-known)**

**Headline:** Stop scrambling for something to post.

**Subheadline:** Plan your entire social calendar in one place, schedule posts to go out automatically, and get back to the work that actually grows your business.

**CTA:** Get started free

---

**Option 3 — Outcome/transformation (works well if you have proof to back it up)**

**Headline:** A consistent social presence — without the daily grind.

**Subheadline:** [Tool name] lets you batch-schedule posts across all your channels in one sitting. Set it once, show up every day.

**CTA:** Try it free — no credit card needed

---

**My recommendation:** Start with Option 1. It's the most specific, answers "what is this?" in under three seconds, and leads with a concrete outcome (20 minutes) rather than a vague promise. If you have a real number — average time saved, number of posts scheduled per week, etc. — swap in the real figure and it will perform even better.

- Who specifically is your core user? (solopreneurs, marketing teams, agencies?)
- Which channels do you support? (naming them — Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok — adds credibility)
- Do you have a free plan or trial? (that changes the CTA)
